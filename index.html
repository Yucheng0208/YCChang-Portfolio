Yu-Cheng Chang

Yu-Cheng Chang is a master’s student in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology. His research focuses on AIoT, embedded systems, and computer vision, strongly emphasizing interdisciplinary innovation and practical integration. He has received numerous national awards for participating in technology competitions and holds several technical inventions. Recently, his work has centered on combining sign language recognition with emotion analysis to enhance accessibility technologies, while actively contributing to technical mentorship and education.
