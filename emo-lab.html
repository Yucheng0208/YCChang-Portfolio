About EMO Lab

EMO Lab is a research team centered on the core concepts of Elements, Modularity, and Optimization. Our members are distributed across Taiwan, from north to south and even to offshore islands, bringing together a group of passionate young researchers and developers who are dedicated to advancing various issues and current affairs through systematic thinking and research outcomes.

We are committed to human-centered approaches, utilizing low-cost computing power and innovative technologies to simplify complex problems. However, "EMO" also represents Emotion. We believe that technology should not merely be intelligent, but should also possess warmth, bringing more softness, kindness, and inclusivity to life.

We are not affiliated with any physical institution, but rather a group of ordinary people brought together by shared dreams. With our vision for the future, we hope to do everything to the best of our ability, making the world more livable—especially during those most "EMO" moments.

Our Principles

Interdisciplinary Innovation

In this era of technological prosperity, cross-disciplinary work and engaging with cutting-edge technological systems is our constant goal. We integrate external needs (societal issues) and internal needs (personal achievement and interests) by viewing the world with an open heart.

Mindful Resource Management

Money is for living—without greed but with purpose, there are no worries. We invest in different equipment, various fields, and cover travel and salaries as needed. When there's abundance, we avoid drowning in wealth; when fame comes, we stay grounded in reality.

Cultivate Mind, Then Skills

We believe in staying true to ourselves and helping others along the way. First, we cultivate the mind; then we master skills. When we achieve success, we never forget our roots, never forget to cultivate the mind, and never forget to continue learning.
